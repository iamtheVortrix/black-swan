About Us

9 Years of Trust,
One Salon at a Time

BS
Est. 2016

Black Swan Unisex Salon has been the go-to destination for beauty and hair care in Kurla West since 2016. Founded by Hiren Sir, our salon was built on one simple belief — every client deserves honest advice, expert hands, and results they love.

We are a team of trained, passionate stylists who consult before we cut, listen before we suggest, and care about your hair as much as you do. From everyday haircuts to advanced nanoplastia treatments — we do it all, and we do it right.

Consultation before every service — we listen first
Trained, certified stylists only — no beginners on your hair
100% formaldehyde-free treatments — your health matters
